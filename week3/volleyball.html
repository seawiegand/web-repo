Travel tournaments

Austin

Typically our first travel tournament of the season is to Austin, TX. Some
of the best competition in the nation goes to this tournament and it is a
great way to start off the season along with some team bonding.

Anaheim

Next in the season is the Anaheim, CA tournament. This one is also always
a blast. The Austin tournament is often like a warmup to see what the competition
is like, but Anaheim is closer to nationals and is a glimpse of how national will
be for the team.

St. Louis

The last tournament of the season is nationals. Every year the location for nationals
changes, but most recently it was in St. Louis, MO. This is where we put toegther
the whole seasons worth of work and where we play our best. At the end of the tournamnet
all the teams who didn't make it to the finals gather and cheer on the final games.
It is truly something to be a part of. Then of course the finals are followed by player
parties and fun.
